STARTUP

Youth Entrepreneurship

Start Up is designed to promote business ownership as a viable career option to middle school and high school youth. Over a 15 week course, youth will create and plan a business of their very own. Once their business plan is complete, youth will pitch their idea to panel of successful business owners who will then partner with youth to help develop their business even further.

WHAT YOUTH LEARN

Entrepreneurship

Financial Management

Professional Development

Register a business

Create a budget

Build relationships and establish boundaries

Brand products and services

Build credit and request loans

Present a professional self image
